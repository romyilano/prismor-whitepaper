Page 02

Purpose:
Show the scale and speed of the AI threat landscape — 89% surge in AI-driven attacks, 29-minute propagation windows, the LiteLLM supply-chain incident reaching tens of thousands of installs in 40 minutes — so the reader understands why the dilemma on page 1 is urgent.

Audience note:
Assumes the reader has absorbed page 1's framing. No security background required.

Panel Count:
6

Image Prompt:

Create a six-panel educational manga page.

Style:
High-contrast noir manga.
Black and white, heavy blacks, dramatic chiaroscuro shadows.
Sharp inked linework, moody cyberpunk atmosphere.
Rain-slicked digital city streets, glowing terminal windows, neon kanji signage.
Diagrams rendered as glowing holographic schematics integrated into scenes.
Not soft. Not cute. Not low-contrast. Not pastel.

Characters in this page:
- SHADOW: A flickering, semi-transparent figure — almost human but never fully resolved. Face is a blur of static. Edges are unstable, like a bad transmission. Wears a courier's jacket in this scene, imitating a legitimate package delivery. Leaves no shadow of its own.

Setting:
The city streets at night, seen from above and at ground level alternating. Shadow moves through a network of data pipelines rendered as glowing underground tunnels and rooftop conduits. Holographic incident-report overlays float above the streets like crime-scene markers.

Panel 1:
Wide aerial view of the city. Neon light reflects off wet rooftops. Below the streets, Shadow moves through a data conduit — a faint distortion, barely visible. A floating holographic overlay above the street reads: "AI-DRIVEN ATTACKS +89% · 2025–2026 · CrowdStrike" in clean terminal font. Caption: "While the Architect deliberated, the city was already under pressure."

Panel 2:
Ground-level. Shadow emerges from a data conduit in the alley, taking on the shape of a courier with a package. A stopwatch hologram floats nearby, frozen at 0:29. Caption: "The average attack propagated in twenty-nine minutes." Second caption: "The fastest completed in twenty-seven seconds."

Panel 3:
Close-up on Shadow's face — or where the face should be. Static and blur where features would be. It holds a package labeled "litellm-utils v2.1.3" in clean font. The package radiates a faint dark aura, invisible unless you're looking for it. Caption: "In March 2026, a poisoned package entered the standard registries."

Panel 4:
Time-lapse panel rendered as a vertical sequence of three mini-frames within the panel — like a film strip at the right edge. Frame one: the package enters one server. Frame two: it spreads to a dozen. Frame three: a counter reads "40,000+ installations" against a dark background. Caption below all three frames: "Forty thousand machines ran it before anyone confirmed it was malicious."

Panel 5:
Wide city shot. Dozens of Shadow-copies flicker between buildings, through data lines, across rooftops — the same distorted static-faced figure multiplied. Holographic alerts blink in windows: "UNAUTHORIZED AGENT DETECTED" and "CREDENTIAL LEAK" and "DEPENDENCY COMPROMISED." Caption: "The attacks were not slow and deliberate. They scaled the way code scales — instantly, and everywhere at once."

Panel 6:
Return to the Architect's loft window, seen from outside in the rain. His silhouette is visible, still at the terminal. The incident reports glow in the distance behind him. Caption: "This was not a theoretical risk. It was already the operating environment." Second caption: "The question was whether the developer's next move would happen inside it or ahead of it."

Lettering:
Leave clear space for English captions in top and bottom caption boxes. Holographic overlays in panels use terminal-style monospace type; distinguish from caption boxes by lighter outline style.

Output:
A single finished manga page.
