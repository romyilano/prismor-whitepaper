Page 03

Purpose:
Reveal the hidden identity-debt problem: machine identities outnumber humans 80:1 in typical enterprises, 97% carry excessive permissions, only 16% of organizations can detect agent-specific threats, and 82% have already discovered unauthorized agents running in their environments.

Audience note:
Assumes the reader understands that AI agents hold credentials and act on behalf of organizations.

Panel Count:
6

Image Prompt:

Create a six-panel educational manga page.

Style:
High-contrast noir manga.
Black and white, heavy blacks, dramatic chiaroscuro shadows.
Sharp inked linework, moody cyberpunk atmosphere.
Rain-slicked digital city streets, glowing terminal windows, neon kanji signage.
Diagrams rendered as glowing holographic schematics integrated into scenes.
Not soft. Not cute. Not low-contrast. Not pastel.

Characters in this page:
- KIRA: Short-cropped dark hair with faint circuit-trace highlights at the temples. Cyan-glowing eyes. Dark courier's trench coat with luminous circuitry stitched into the lining. Fingerless gloves. Small data-port scar on left wrist.
- THE ARCHITECT: Mid-forties. Tired eyes behind wire-frame glasses. Salt-and-pepper stubble. Worn flannel overshirt. Coffee cup at hand.

Setting:
A vast underground identity registry — a cavernous server hall rendered as a city block seen from below, with rows of glowing ID cards suspended in the air like lit windows in a skyscraper. Most glow ghostly white (machine identities). A small cluster glows warm amber (human identities). Kira and the Architect stand on a walkway overlooking the grid.

Panel 1:
Wide establishing shot from above: the vast credential grid. Hundreds of cards glow in rows. Most are featureless white, barely distinguishable from each other. Caption: "The problem did not begin with the attacks. It began with the infrastructure the attacks were aimed at."

Panel 2:
The Architect stands at a railing, looking down at the grid. Kira is beside him, one hand on the rail. He gestures across the rows. Dialogue: "For every human identity in a typical enterprise, eighty machines hold credentials." Holographic ratio marker floats beside him — a clean schematic showing one amber card and eighty white ones in a column.

Panel 3:
Close-up on several white cards in the grid — machine identities. Each one has a name (AGENT-DEPLOY-7, BUILD-RUNNER-3, DATA-FETCH-12) and a long permissions list. Most of the list is grayed out, unused. Caption: "Ninety-seven percent of those machine identities carry permissions they have never exercised and will never need." Caption: "Excessive permission is not malice. It is default."

Panel 4:
Medium shot. Kira looks at the cards, her circuit-trace highlights faintly active. Thought balloon (styled as a terminal readout rather than a cloud): "Only sixteen percent of organizations would know if one of these — if I — had been compromised mid-operation."

Panel 5:
Wide shot of the grid. In the far corner of the cavern, a cluster of cards glows an unregistered, eerie color — neither amber nor white. Small holographic alert tag above them reads: "UNAUTHORIZED · SOURCE UNKNOWN." Caption: "Eighty-two percent of enterprises have already discovered AI agents operating in their environments with no record of who deployed them or why."

Panel 6:
The Architect and Kira face each other on the walkway. The grid looms behind both of them, vast and mostly dark. The Architect speaks; his voice is measured, not panicked. Dialogue: "The issue isn't that the agents are untrustworthy." Beat panel — a narrow additional beat between them showing Kira waiting. Dialogue continuing: "It's that we built the entire grid before we had a way to watch it."

Lettering:
Caption boxes at top and bottom panel borders. Thought balloon for Kira styled as a terminal window with cursor, distinct from standard speech balloons. Holographic labels in panels use small monospace type with a faint box outline.

Output:
A single finished manga page.
